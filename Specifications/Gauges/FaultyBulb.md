###FaultyBulb

- **Description**: if any of your bulbs are faulty or blown, this light will warn you that one of the exterior bulbs has an issue.
Most modern vehicles will tell you which bulb it is.
- **Values:**: __0__: No bulb failure, the LED is off.
               __1__: There is an issue with the headlamp leveling or AFS, the LED is flashing.
               __2__: There is a light bulb failure, the LED is on/solid.
- **Calculated**: No
- **Type**: Led
- **Priority**: 1
- **Vocal Alert**: No
