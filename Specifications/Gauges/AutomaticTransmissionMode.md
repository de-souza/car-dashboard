### AutomaticTransmissionModes

- **Description:** 8 Indicator lights representing the modes of the automatic
driving : Park (P), Reverse (R), Drive (D), Neutral (N), First (1 or L [Low]),
Second (2 or S), Third (3), Overdrive (D or OD).
- **Values:** 0, 1
- **Calculated:** No
- **Vocal alert:** No
- **Type:** LED 
- **Priority:** 0
